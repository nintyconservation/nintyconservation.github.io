FF (????? FF) is a dual-type "Normal" (0x80)/"Normal" 0x8F glitch Pokémon in Pokémon Crystal, with an index number of hex:FF (dec:255).

This glitch Pokémon and ????? (Gold/Silver FF) can be used for the Time Capsule exploit.

Methods to obtain

  1. Stabilize a bad clone to obtain (00), have a party of six Pokémon; and then bring the (00) to the top of the party. Then use move PkMn without Mail to bring another Pokémon to the top and take the into the Day Care Center on Route 34 and out. This will convert the into a (FF).
  2. ????? party overloading roaming Pokémon manipulation (255 Attack).
  3. Arbitrary code execution (e.g. through 0x1500 control code arbitrary code execution, TM33 outside of the TM/HM pocket; glitch Pokédex mode 9).
  4. Trade ????? (FF) from Pokémon Gold and Silver.

Notes

This glitch Pokémon can be used to hide other Pokémon below it on the deposit and move PkMn w/o Mail menus, making it applicable for ????? party overloading.
