ゥ pゥ, (hex:36, dec:054) is a glitch Trainer class in Pokémon Yellow.

The equivalent glitch Trainer in Pokémon Red and Blue is Pk (hex:36).

It can be fought with the Ditto trick with a Special stat of 254 (hex:FE).
