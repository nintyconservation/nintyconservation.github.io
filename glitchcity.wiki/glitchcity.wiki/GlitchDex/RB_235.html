Glitch Pokémon (0xEB) is a dual-type Normal/Flying-type glitch Pokémon in Pokémon Red and Blue.

It is a hybrid of Dodrio, and one of the scrambled Bulbasaur glitch Pokémon in Pokémon Red, Blue, and Yellow, the others being /PkMn ▼PkMn and ゥ 4- 4.

. 4( h 4 is the equivalent trade glitch Pokémon in Pokémon Yellow.

This glitch Pokémon is an arbitrary learnset glitch Pokémon that takes level-up and evolution data from VRAM $992B.

It cannot be caught with the Trainer escape glitch or another means of instant encounter, as a Misty battle will be initiated instead.
