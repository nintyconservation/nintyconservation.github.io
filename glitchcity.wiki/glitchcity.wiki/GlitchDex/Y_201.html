ァ / g J 1 is a dual-type Poison-type glitch Pokémon in Pokémon Yellow.

It is a hybrid of Nidorino, and shares Nidorino's sprites due to sharing both the same sprite pointers and the same sprite bank.

'◣ゥ 8 is the equivalent trade glitch Pokémon in Pokémon Red and Blue.

It cannot be caught with the Trainer escape glitch or another means of instant encounter, as a Youngster battle will be initiated instead.

Methods to obtain

  • Party remaining HP glitch or storage box remaining HP glitch with a remaining HP of 201.
  • Time Capsule exploit (Furret).
  • LOL glitch (0xC9 sub-tile)
  • International fossil conversion glitch with an Attack stat of 201.
  • Equivalent trade of ◣ゥ 8 from Pokémon Red and Blue.
  • Arbitrary code execution
