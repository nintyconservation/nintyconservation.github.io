ゥ (F5) is a dual-type Glitch (0x1C)/Glitch (0x37)-type glitch Pokémon in Pokémon Red and Blue.

Its family number is 255.

▼pゥ is the equivalent trade glitch Pokémon in Pokémon Yellow.

It cannot be encountered with the Trainer escape glitch or another means of instant encounter, as a Channeler battle will be initiated instead.

Both its front and back sprite are unstable, which results in a freeze, which can be fixed by freezing the sound bank values in place, and if it's applied, its front sprite behavior is identical to 4 4. However, it crashes after it partially shows up.

The back sprite glitches out the current music in an constant manner and then when it gets sent out, it monotones the audio, but gameplay still resumes.
