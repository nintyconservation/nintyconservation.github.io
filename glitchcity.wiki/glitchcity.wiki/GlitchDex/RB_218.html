B (DA) is a dual-type Rock/Ground-type glitch Pokémon in Pokémon Red and Blue.

It is a hybrid of Onix.

] (DA) is the equivalent trade glitch Pokémon in Pokémon Yellow.

It cannot be caught with the Trainer escape glitch or another means of instant encounter, as a Beauty battle will be initiated instead.

Methods to obtain

  • Party remaining HP glitch or storage box remaining HP glitch with a remaining HP of 218.
  • Time Capsule exploit (Mareep).
  • CoolTrainer♀ corruption (0xDA sub-tile).
  • LOL glitch (0xDA sub-tile)
  • International fossil conversion glitch with an Attack stat of 218.
  • Double distort CoolTrainer♀ corruption (0xDA sub-tile, normally cannot be input)
  • Equivalent trade of ] (DA) from Pokémon Yellow.
  • Arbitrary code execution
