/ᴘᴋᴍɴ ▼ᴘᴋᴍɴÙ is a glitch Pokémon in Pokémon Red and Blue (Spanish).

It is a hybrid of Shellder.

Í 8 ´dp ァ is the equivalent trade glitch Pokémon in Pokémon Yellow.

it cannot be caught with the Trainer escape glitch or another means of instant encounter, as a Blackbelt battle will be initiated instead.
