'Ng ゥ₽ (F8) 94 h (F9) Glitch Pokémon (FA)

94 h is a dual-type Normal (0x80)/Ghost (0x88)-type glitch Pokémon in Pokémon Red and Blue.

Its family number is 213. It is similar to 94.

₽ (F9) is the equivalent trade glitch Pokémon in Pokémon Yellow.

It is an arbitrary learnset glitch Pokémon that takes level-up and evolution data from VRAM $9A20.

It cannot be caught with the Trainer escape glitch or another means of instant encounter, as a 1▶ゥ· (0x31) glitch Trainer battle will be initiated instead.

Methods to obtain

  • Party remaining HP glitch or storage box remaining HP glitch with a remaining HP of 249.
  • Time Capsule exploit (Granbull).
  • CoolTrainer♀ corruption ("3" character).

  • International fossil conversion glitch with an Attack stat of 249.
  • Double distort CoolTrainer♀ corruption ("3" character)
  • Equivalent trade of ₽ (F9) from Pokémon Yellow.
