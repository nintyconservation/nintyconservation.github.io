Z ゥ (E5) is a Normal-type glitch Pokémon in Pokémon Yellow.

It is a hybrid of Persian.

C (E5) is the equivalent trade glitch Pokémon in Pokémon Red and Blue.

It cannot be caught with the Trainer escape glitch or another means of instant encounter, as a Giovanni battle will be initiated instead.

  • Party remaining HP glitch or storage box remaining HP glitch with a remaining HP of 229.
  • Time Capsule exploit (Aipom).
  • LOL glitch ("'m" character)
  • International fossil conversion glitch with an Attack stat of 229.
  • Equivalent trade of C (E5) from Pokémon Red and Blue.
  • Arbitrary code execution
