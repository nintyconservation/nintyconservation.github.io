Glitch Pokémon (0xE4) is a dual-type Bug/Glitch (0xC8)-type glitch Pokémon in Pokémon Red and Blue.

Its family number is 175.

8B 4 8 is the equivalent trade glitch Pokémon in Pokémon Yellow.

This glitch Pokémon has a name that is terminated with 0x50 before it begins, essentially giving it a 'blank' name when the name has to be displayed. However, other characters exist after the 0x50 byte.

It cannot be caught with the Trainer escape glitch or another means of instant encounter, as a Scientist battle will be initiated instead.

As glitch Pokémon 0xE4's front sprite is sourced at CDE5 in WRAM (in the middle of the last saved tile data), the front sprite of this glitch Pokémon will vary based on the screen data last saved when opening a menu like the Pokémon or items menu. See arbitrary sprites for more information.
