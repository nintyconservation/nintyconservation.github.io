ゥ (F3) is an Electric-type glitch Pokémon in Pokémon Red and Blue.

It is a hybrid of Raichu.

ゥ▶ ゥ▼ ゥ is the equivalent trade glitch Pokémon in Pokémon Yellow.

It cannot be caught with the Trainer escape glitch or another means of instant encounter, as a Rival (champion variation) battle will be initiated instead.

Methods to obtain

  • Party remaining HP glitch or storage box remaining HP glitch with a remaining HP of 243.
  • Time Capsule exploit (Pineco).
  • CoolTrainer♀ corruption ("/" character).
  • LOL glitch ("/" character)
  • International fossil conversion glitch with an Attack stat of 243.
  • Double distort CoolTrainer♀ corruption ("/" character)
  • Equivalent trade of ゥ▶ ゥ▼ ゥ from Pokémon Yellow.
  • Arbitrary code execution
