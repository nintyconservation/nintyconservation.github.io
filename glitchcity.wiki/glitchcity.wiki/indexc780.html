(Variable name) is a false Water (0x95)-type glitch move in Pokémon Red and Blue. It is capable of causing Super Glitch.

Successfully using this glitch move without any significant effects from Super Glitch (possible by having a 0x50 sub-tile early in the saved screen data) may result in a damaging move with Pound's animation which may paralyse the foe.
