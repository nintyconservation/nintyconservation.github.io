These are various "Dex" database ideas for the Glitch City Laboratories wiki. Feel free to discuss any additional ideas in this thread.

Additional

These are databases that are less thorough compared to the ones above, or incomplete.

  • Extended GlitchDex: A list of glitch Pokémon in various language versions of the games
  • AreaDex: Glitch location data
  • ValidSourceMapGlitchCityDex: For every real location in Pokémon Red, Blue, and Yellow, provide a screenshot of the first 256 warps to that map (Safari Zone exit glitch loads warp 0x04)). Also document D35E/F (D35D/E in Yellow) pointer and give Move 0x00 corruption (Generation I)/LOL glitch etc. species to catch output/or if freezes the game.
  • ItemDex: A database of glitch items in various language versions of the games
  • AttackDex: A database of glitch moves
  • AttackEffectDex: Move effect data including unused/glitch move effects
  • BadgeDescriptionDex: Badge describer options
  • BattleAnimationDex: Glitch animation data
  • BattleTypeDex: A database of invalid D057/D056 values in Pokémon Red, Blue, and Yellow; which usually control whether the player is on the overworld, in a wild battle or in a Trainer battle
  • BerryDex: Glitch Berry data
  • BigDollDex: A database of big glitch dolls in Pokémon Gold, Silver and Crystal
  • DayDex: Glitch days of the week
  • DefaultNameDex: Glitch preset names in Pokémon Red, Blue, and Yellow
  • DexDex: A database of glitch Pokédex sortings
  • DollDex: A database of glitch dolls in Pokémon Gold, Silver and Crystal
  • ElevatorDex: A database of out of bounds lift destinations
  • ExpGroupDex: A database of glitch experience groups
  • FacingDex: Invalid 0xC109 address values in Pokémon Red, Blue, and Yellow
  • FamilyDex: Unused pokemon families in Pokémon Red, Blue, and Yellow
  • FieldMoveDex: A database of glitch field moves
  • HitFlagDex: Invalid D05E ('hit flag', i.e. normal, critical hit, or one-hit KO) values in Pokémon Red, Blue, and Yellow
  • MetascriptDex: Invalid map scripts in Pokémon Red, Blue, and Yellow, see glitch meta-map script activation
  • OverworldTileDex: Gallery of overworld tiles and building blocks
  • PhoneDex: A database of glitch contact numbers in Pokémon Gold, Silver and Crystal
  • PlayerPaletteDex: Glitch palettes for the player character in Pokémon Crystal
  • PosterDex: A database of glitch posters in Pokémon Gold, Silver and Crystal
  • PredefDex: Predefined commands. See also: Pokémon Yellow predefined functions list.
  • PrizeDex: Game Corner prizes.
  • RadioDex?: Glitch radio channels
  • RibbonDex: A database of glitch ribbons
  • SlotSymbolDex: A database of glitch slot machine symbols
  • SoundbankDex: Glitch sound bank data in Pokémon Red, Blue, and Yellow
  • SpecialEmoteDex: Glitch special Pikachu emote data in Pokémon Yellow
  • StatDex: A database of glitch stats
  • TMHMDex: A database of glitch 'wrong pocket' TMs and HMs in Pokémon Gold, Silver and Crystal
  • TraderDex: A database of glitch traders
  • TrainerDex: A database of glitch trainers
  • TypeDex: A database of glitch types
  • UnownDex: A database of glitch Unown
  • BattleWeatherDex: Glitch battle weather data in Pokémon Gold, Silver and Crystal
  • OverworldWeatherDex?: Glitch overworld weather data if possible in Pokémon Ruby, Sapphire and Pokémon Emerald
