Bonsly's summary

Bonsly refers to a valid Generation IV Pokémon.

Prior to Generation IV Bonsly also appears in Pokémon XD: Gale of Darkness as both an NPC character and as a Pokémon available to battle with in Realgam Tower's Battle Bingo minigame, where it only knows Rock Slide.

By using a cheating device Bonsly may also be obtained in the party where it serves as a glitch Pokémon. Unfortunately, attempting to trade the 0x019D ?????????? from a Generation III game on to Pokémon XD will not work, as the party checker considers it an illegal Pokémon.

Action Replay codes (PAL)

These codes replace the first Pokémon in the party with Bonsly.

CN1X-UR77-05ZJC

4G9V-Q7BU-EMBUM
