Name: (Unknown)

Identifier (HEX): 0x26

Pokédex sorting 0x26 is a glitch Pokédex sorting in Pokémon Gold and Silver.

Effect

  • Unknown effect/game does not freeze
  • Pressing Select on the sorting may result in a white screen freeze

When accessed with C6D8 method

  • May cause arbitrary code execution at region D5EA in RAM when chosen
