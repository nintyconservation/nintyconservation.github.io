The position at Silph Co. 5F in which the player can become trapped. Source: scresadoo0 and PokeTips

The Silph Co. trapped glitch, also known as the Team Rocket glitch is a glitch in Pokémon: Let's Go, Pikachu! and Let's Go, Eevee!

Summary

This glitch takes place before Team Rocket have been cleared from Silph Co.

Going to Silph Co.'s 5F and standing at a specific corner of the room has the potential of blocking the player, leaving them unable to move out of the spot.

Further details are unknown, other than the player may not truly be trapped forever after saving, as the player may escape by sending a Support Trainer via co-op mode.
