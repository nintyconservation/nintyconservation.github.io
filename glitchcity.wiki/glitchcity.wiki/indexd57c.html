4( h 4 ?₽ (E8) is a Water-type glitch Pokémon in Pokémon Yellow.

It is a hybrid of Seaking, and has the same sprites as Seaking due to sharing the same sprite pointers, dimensions and sprite bank.

X C (E8) is the equivalent trade glitch Pokémon in Pokémon Red and Blue.

It cannot be caught with the Trainer escape glitch or another means of instant encounter, as a CoolTrainer♀ battle will be initiated instead.
