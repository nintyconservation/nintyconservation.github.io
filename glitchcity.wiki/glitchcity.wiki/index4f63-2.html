The row of tiles in question

In Pokémon Diamond and Pearl and Pokémon Platinum, there is a row of tiles on Route 224 that, if Seabreak Path is not open, all act as water tiles despite their appearance.

  • Discovered by Jimiscol of Bulbagarden Forums
