Pゥ ゥ ゥ is a dual-type Normal (0x8D)/Ground (0x84)-type glitch Pokémon in Pokémon Red and Blue.

Its family number is 205.

It has an unstable front-sprite, which causes this glitch Pokémon to freeze the game when sent out on the opponent's side.

'ゥ. (D5) is the equivalent trade glitch Pokémon in Pokémon Yellow.

It cannot be encountered directly with the Trainer escape glitch or another means of instant encounter, as a Juggler (variation 1) battle will be initiated instead.
