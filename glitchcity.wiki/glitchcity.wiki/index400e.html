₽ (F9) is a dual-type Normal (0x11)/Glitch (0x25) glitch Pokémon in Pokémon Yellow.

Its family number is 000.

94 h is the equivalent trade glitch Pokémon in Pokémon Red and Blue.

It cannot be caught with the Trainer escape glitch or another means of instant encounter, as a "1▶ゥ· (31)" glitch Trainer battle will be initiated instead.

Methods to obtain

  • Party remaining HP glitch or storage box remaining HP glitch with a remaining HP of 249.
  • Time Capsule exploit (Granbull).
  • LOL glitch ("3" character)
  • International fossil conversion glitch with an Attack stat of 249.
  • Equivalent trade of 94 h from Pokémon Red and Blue.
  • Arbitrary code execution

Evolution(s)

This glitch Pokémon is an arbitrary learnset glitch Pokémon that takes level-up and evolution data from VRAM $9A20.

Pokédex data

₽'s Pokédex data is not constant and is sourced from F403 in Echo RAM (the equivalent of D403 in WRAM); which is in the middle of the wWarpEntries database. It can also be manipulated by moving specific items to item 116, which makes it an ideal execution point for arbitrary code execution without bootstrap Pokémon (unlike the "ws m (hex:63)" glitch item which would normally require a bootstrap stored Pokémon setup).
