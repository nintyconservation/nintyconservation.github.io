Glitch Pokémon (DC) 7PkMn 'v (DD) PkMn (DE)

7PkMn 'v is a dual-type Water/Poison-type glitch Pokémon in Pokémon Red and Blue.

It is a hybrid of Tentacruel. Due to this glitch Pokémon and Tentacruel both sharing the same sprite pointers and sprite bank, its front and back sprites appear as exact copies of Tentacruel's sprites.

4, ゥァ (DD) is the equivalent trade glitch Pokémon in Pokémon Yellow.

It cannot be caught with the Trainer escape glitch or another means of instant encounter, as a Juggler (variation 2) battle will be initiated instead.

Methods to obtain

  • Party remaining HP glitch or storage box remaining HP glitch with a remaining HP of 221.
  • Time Capsule exploit (Bellossom).
  • CoolTrainer♀ corruption (0xDD sub-tile).
  • LOL glitch (0xDD sub-tile)
  • International fossil conversion glitch with an Attack stat of 221.
  • Double distort CoolTrainer♀ corruption (0xDD sub-tile, normally cannot be input)
  • Equivalent trade of 4, ゥァ (DD) from Pokémon Yellow.
  • Arbitrary code execution
