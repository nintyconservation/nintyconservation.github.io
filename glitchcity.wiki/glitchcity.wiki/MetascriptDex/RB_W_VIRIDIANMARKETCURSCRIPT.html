This article details various glitch meta-map scripts in Viridian City Poké Mart in Pokémon Red and Blue.

This script is controlled by memory address D60D.

Highlights

  • 0x00: May freeze the game if the player has already delivered Oak's Parcel
  • 0x01: May display a glitch dialog box if the player has already delivered Oak's Parcel
  • 0x03: Upon entering, controls may lock up and NPCs in the map may be missing. Glitch audio may play if activated after the player character has already entered the Poké Mart.
  • 0x04: Walking lag may occur for both the player and NPCs, with a glitched walking animation
  • 0x07: May display a glitch dialog box that freezes the game
  • 0x09: NPCs may disappear and player character may be forced to walk on the spot up and down. Map may become a Glitch City which is visible after opening the menu with Start.
  • 0x0B: Game may freeze. If the player enters the building with this script set, the NPCs may all face down before the game freezes.
  • 0x0F, 0x10: Controls may lock up other than Start unless the menu is already open
  • 0x11, 0x13, 0x14: If the player enters the Poké Mart, the game may freeze before all or most of the NPCs load. If activated while already in the Poké Mart, the game may freeze without the NPCs disappearing.
  • 0x12: Game may freeze at certain moments but not always straight away
