TM36 is a (variable name) (0x28)-type glitch move in Pokémon Red, Blue, and Yellow. It is not capable of causing Super Glitch.

Using this glitch move may deal damage with Gust's animation equal to half the opponent's current HP, or 1 HP if 1 HP is remaining.
