…… is a dual-type Glitch (0xA5)/Glitch (0x3B)-type glitch Pokémon in Pokémon Red and Blue.

Its family number is 245.

Its front sprite after being sent out on the opposing Pokémon's side freezes the game.

When sound bank fixes are applied, its behavior is identical to 4 4, but it crashes after it partially shows up.

[h4to89 is the equivalent trade glitch Pokémon in Pokémon Yellow.

It cannot be encountered directly with the Trainer escape glitch or another means of instant encounter, as a Prof. Oak battle will be initiated instead.
