ゥ (C1) is a dual-type Normal (0x8D)/Ground (0x84)-type glitch Pokémon in Pokémon Red and Blue.

Its family number is 205.

It has an unstable front-sprite, which causes this glitch Pokémon to freeze the game when sent out on the opponent's side.

♀ . is the equivalent trade glitch Pokémon in Pokémon Yellow.

Methods to obtain

  • Equivalent trade of ♀ . from Pokémon Yellow.
  • Party remaining HP glitch or storage box remaining HP glitch with a remaining HP of 193.
  • Time Capsule exploit (Meganium).
  • CoolTrainer♀ corruption (0xC1 sub-tile).
  • LOL glitch (0xC1 sub-tile)
  • International fossil conversion glitch with an Attack stat of 193.
  • Double distort CoolTrainer♀ corruption (0xC1 sub-tile, normally cannot be entered)
  • Old man trick ("Ö" (German version only)).
  • Arbitrary code execution
