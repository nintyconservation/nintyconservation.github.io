This glitch allows players to get past the Marowak ghost in the Pokémon Tower in Pokémon Red, Blue, and Yellow, regardless of whether the Silph Scope is in the item pack or not.

Procedure

  1. Enter Pokemon Tower, and go to the stairs that lead to the final level.
  2. After the Ghost appears, players should use a Poke Doll. It will flee from the battle.
