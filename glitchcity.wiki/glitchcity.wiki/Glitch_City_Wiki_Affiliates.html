Glitch City Laboratories is happily accepting affiliation requests for other Pokémon websites.

What this means is we add a link to your website both on this page and on the sidebar, provided your website includes a link back to Glitch City Laboratories.

If your website would like to request an affiliation, you can bring it up in this thread on the forums, send an email to Abwayax ([email protected]) or Torchickens ([email protected]) or send them a private message on the forums.

If you would like to use an affiliation button, feel free to embed one of the following icons on to your website, of your choice.

Current affiliates

Legendary Star Blob 2 (Hakuda)

Legendary Star Blob 2 (Japanese: 伝説のスターブロブ２) is a Japanese video game website under the domain hakuda2 of FC2 Website hosting, with excellent game strategy sub-sites including very detailed pages related to glitches and curiosities in Pokémon Red, Green, Blue, and Yellow and Pokémon Gold, Silver and Crystal. In Japanese, the phrase "haku da" (はくだ) can literally be interpreted as "it's a hack".

You can visit them here! http://hakuda2.web.fc2.com/

Red/Green/Blue/Yellow coverage: http://hakuda2.web.fc2.com/wario/poke1/mo.html

Gold/Silver/Crystal coverage: http://hakuda2.web.fc2.com/wario/poke3/mo.html


Pokémon Speedruns

Pokémon Speedruns is an English wiki and forum for instructions on speedrunning Pokémon. You can find fascinating information here related to obscure and often elaborate glitches in the Pokémon games related to speedrunning, and is a place for people both well-known in and new to the Pokémon speedrunning community to talk about speedrunning Pokémon.

You can visit them here!
Wiki: http://wiki.pokemonspeedruns.com/index.php?title=Main_Page
Forums: http://forums.pokemonspeedruns.com/index.php

PRAMA Initiative

PRAMA Initiative is a French Pokémon glitch website and forum managed by Krys3000. It is an excellent site and the place to go for Pokémon glitch coverage in French!

You can visit them here!
Main site: http://www.prama-initiative.com/
Forum: http://forum.prama-initiative.com/
YouTube: https://www.youtube.com/channel/UCYdPjsAzUytNYPhzCrRS1mw

MissingNo. Glitch City

MissingNo. Glitch City is an Italian Pokémon glitch affiliate managed by Yayo1990, originally by three staff members in 2008. The forum and a website was later resurrected in 2019. It also has an official YouTube channel.

You can find many glitches here detailed in Italian, with many more likely to be added in the future. So have fun reading about glitches and secrets here if Italian is your native tongue (of course, the website is also available for non-Italian speakers; if you like)!

You can visit them here!
Main site: http://missingnoglitch.altervista.org/
Forum: https://missingnoglitch.forumcommunity.net/
YouTube: https://www.youtube.com/channel/UCVo-GTRDfqsKnLhBTrCN5hg
