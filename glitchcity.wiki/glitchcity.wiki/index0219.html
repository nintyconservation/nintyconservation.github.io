Pokédex sorting 0xB3 is a glitch Pokédex sorting in Pokémon Gold and Silver.

Effect

  • Unknown effect/game does not freeze after opening the sorting
  • Pressing Select may bring up mode selection screen, where mode has no description. Unknown effect from here/pressing A on mode selection screen does not freeze the game.

When accessed with C6D8 method

  • Unknown effect/pressing A does not freeze the game
