Super Smash Bros. for Nintendo 3DS is the fourth entry in the Super Smash Bros. series of Nintendo themed fighting games. It is paired with Super Smash Bros. for Wii U, which was released later, although both of these games were announced at the same time.

Super Star music lingering glitch

A glitch on the WarioWare, Inc. stage in which the Super Star music continues to play even when no character is invincible. This glitch was documented by Johans Nidorino, but ChickasaurusGL was later able to record it from her capture card.

It is not known whether this glitch has been patched.
