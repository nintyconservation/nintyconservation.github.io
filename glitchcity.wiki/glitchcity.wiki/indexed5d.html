PkMn pゥ ゥ (DC) is a dual-type Normal (0x91)/[D030] (0x50) type glitch Pokémon in Pokémon Yellow.

Its family number is 202.

Glitch Pokémon 0xDC is the equivalent trade glitch Pokémon in Pokémon Red and Blue.

It cannot be caught with the Trainer escape glitch or another means of instant encounter, as a Rocker battle will be initiated instead.

Methods to obtain

  • Party remaining HP glitch or storage box remaining HP glitch with a remaining HP of 220.
  • Time Capsule exploit (Ampharos).
  • LOL glitch (0xDC sub-tile)
  • International fossil conversion glitch with an Attack stat of 220.
  • Equivalent trade of Glitch Pokémon (DC) from Pokémon Red and Blue.
  • Arbitrary code execution
