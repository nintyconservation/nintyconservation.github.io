Super Glitch describes a number of buffer-overflow related glitches in the Pokémon games, and the access points for these corruption (e.g. "Super Glitch moves"), most commonly for Pokémon Red, Blue, and Yellow, but also in Pokémon Ruby, Sapphire and Pokémon Emerald, Pokémon FireRed and LeafGreen and possibly Generation IV.
