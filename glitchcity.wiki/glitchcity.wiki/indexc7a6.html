DollDex

From Glitch City Wiki

Glitch dolls are glitch variations of the doll decorations in Pokémon Gold, Silver and Crystal.

Procedure

In order to 'own' a particular glitch doll the player may alter either address D95E to place a doll on the left side of the table or the address D95F to place a doll on the right side of the table. Below is a table of some of the possible values that the player can use in Pokémon Gold and Silver.
