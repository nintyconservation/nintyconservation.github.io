This category holds glitches from non-core series Pokémon games.

Currently, "non-core series Pokémon games" are considered to be Pokémon games which do not follow the badge, Hall of Fame and Pokédex quest. Pokémon games not found on handheld consoles such as Pokémon Colosseum may be found on this list.
