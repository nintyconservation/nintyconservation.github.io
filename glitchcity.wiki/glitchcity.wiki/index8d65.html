ゥ .4 is a dual-type PokéManiac (0x61)/Glitch (0x27)-type glitch Pokémon in Pokémon Red and Blue.

Its family number is 234.

p PkMn p' ' is the equivalent trade glitch Pokémon in Pokémon Yellow.

Methods to obtain

  • Ditto trick (194 Special)
  • Equivalent trade of p PkMn p' ' from Pokémon Yellow.
  • Party remaining HP glitch or storage box remaining HP glitch with a remaining HP of 194.
  • Time Capsule exploit (Cyndaquil).
  • CoolTrainer♀ corruption (0xC2 sub-tile).
  • LOL glitch (0xC2 sub-tile)
  • International fossil conversion glitch with an Attack stat of 194.
  • Double distort CoolTrainer♀ corruption (0xC2 sub-tile, normally cannot be entered)
  • Old man trick (Ü character (German version only, enterable), or 0xC2 sub-tile (normally cannot be entered))
  • Arbitrary code execution
