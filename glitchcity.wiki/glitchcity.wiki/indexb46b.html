Name: TM31

Identifier (HEX): DF
Identifier (DEC): 223
Effect pointer: 0428

TM31 is a wrong pocket TM in Pokémon Crystal. Its description is "Reduces the foe's accuracy.".

Effect

Using TM31 may freeze the game.
