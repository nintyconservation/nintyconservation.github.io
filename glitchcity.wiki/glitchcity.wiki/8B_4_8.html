8B 4 8 is a dual-type Glitch (0x39)/Normal-type glitch Pokémon in Pokémon Yellow.

Its family number is 250.

Glitch Pokémon (0xE4) is the equivalent trade glitch Pokémon in Pokémon Red and Blue.

It cannot be caught with the Trainer escape glitch or another means of instant encounter, as a Scientist battle will be initiated instead.
