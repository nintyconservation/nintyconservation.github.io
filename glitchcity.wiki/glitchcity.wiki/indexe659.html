c 7 is a dual-type Bug/Glitch (0xC8)-type glitch Pokémon in Pokémon Red and Blue.

Its family number is 175.

4? is the equivalent trade glitch Pokémon in Pokémon Yellow.

It cannot be caught with the Trainer escape glitch or another means of instant encounter, as a Bruno battle will be initiated instead.

As c 7's front sprite is sourced at CDE5 in WRAM (in the middle of the last saved tile data), the front sprite of this glitch Pokémon will vary based on the screen data last saved when opening a menu like the Pokémon or items menu. See arbitrary sprites for more information.

Methods to obtain

  • Party remaining HP glitch or storage box remaining HP glitch with a remaining HP of 233.
  • Time Capsule exploit (Wooper).
  • CoolTrainer♀ corruption ("ァ" character).
  • LOL glitch ("ァ" character)
  • International fossil conversion glitch with an Attack stat of 233.
  • Double distort CoolTrainer♀ corruption ("ァ" character, normally cannot be input)
  • Equivalent trade of 4? from Pokémon Yellow.
