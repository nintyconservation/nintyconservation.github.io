Slowpoke Well out of bounds corruption occurs in the French version of Pokémon Crystal and is a specific variation of out of bounds Glitch City corruption.

When the player steps out of bounds in Slowpoke Well from west of the location below (by cheating or possibly through map corrupting glitches, arbitrary code execution), it is possible for the player to encounter a ?????. The player's item and party data may also suffer great corruption.
