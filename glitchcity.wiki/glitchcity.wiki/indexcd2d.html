3TRAINERPOKé₽ is a dual-type glitch (0xCF)/Normal (0x0E)-type glitch Pokémon in Pokémon Yellow.

'M (00) is the equivalent trade glitch Pokémon in Pokémon Red and Blue.

Methods to obtain

  • Equivalent trade of RB:000 from Red/Blue.
  • Party remaining HP glitch or storage box remaining HP glitch with a remaining HP of 256.
  • Time Capsule exploit (unstable hybrid between 3TrainerPoké₽ and another Pokémon).
  • LOL glitch (00 sub-tile)
  • International fossil conversion glitch with an Attack stat of 256.
  • Evolve .8 (0xCC) into 3TrainerPoké₽ at level 32.
  • Evolve ゥ' B (0xDB) at Level 34.
  • Evolve p [DF] at Level 0.
  • Evolve ▼ pゥ (0xF5) at Level 34.
  • Expanded party encounter table manipulation
  • Arbitrary code execution

Notes

When you initiate a battle with 3TRAINERPOKé, the battle will continue after catching it, but not defeating it. If "3TRAINERPOKé" is captured again, the battle will truly end and the Pokémon will become Ditto.

This glitch Pokémon's encounter flag means that if a 3TRAINERPOKé battle is initiated in the wild, or if 3TRAINERPOKé is sent out by a trainer, then it will duplicate the quantity of item 1 by 128 if less than 128 (unlike MissingNo. and 'M (00) which duplicate item 6 by 128). Currently the only known ways to encounter 3TRAINERPOKé in the wild without a cheating device, hacking or editing the memory are through expanded party encounter table manipulation and arbitrary code execution.
