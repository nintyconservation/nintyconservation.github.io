Glitch Pokémon (0xFA) is a Poison-type glitch Pokémon in Pokémon Red and Blue.

It is a hybrid of Nidorino and uses Nidorino's sprites, due to this glitch Pokémon and Nidorino both sharing the same sprite pointers and using the same sprite bank.

ゥ▼ ‖ゥ♂ is the equivalent trade glitch Pokémon in Pokémon Yellow.

It cannot be caught with the Trainer escape glitch or another means of instant encounter, as a 4 8 : (0x32) glitch Trainer battle will be initiated instead.

Methods to obtain

  • Party remaining HP glitch or storage box remaining HP glitch with a remaining HP of 250.
  • Time Capsule exploit (Qwilfish).
  • CoolTrainer♀ corruption ("4" character).
  • LOL glitch ("4" character)
  • International fossil conversion glitch with an Attack stat of 250.
  • Evolve 'M (FE) at Level 51 (via evolve without an evolutionary stone glitch with p (CB)).
  • Evolve 'M (FE) at Level 201.
  • Double distort CoolTrainer♀ corruption ("4" character)
  • Equivalent trade of ゥ▼ ‖ゥ♂ from Pokémon Yellow.
  • Arbitrary code execution
