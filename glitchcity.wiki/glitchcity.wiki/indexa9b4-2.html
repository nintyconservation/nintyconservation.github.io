Family 152 is an artificial glitch Pokémon family in Pokémon Red and Blue, representing a non-existing glitch Pokémon. It can be found with Game Genie code 980-47A-A2A (replacing Pidgey). This code may also seemingly replace Pidgey's Pokédex entry with Mr. Mime's.

Its sprites are not in the banked area, so it doesn't matter in this case which Pokémon are replaced to form the glitch Pokémon. However, its front sprite may freeze the game. By locking C0EF and C0F0 (sound bank) to a valid value, its front sprite may be seen, but may force a glitch Trainer and glitch battle.
