The unused Sunyshore City house is an unused map in Pokémon Diamond and Pearl.

The map is a small generic, house consisting of a woman (Lass sprite) and man (Sailor sprite), with a television, fridge, sink, bed, table, chair and plant.

If the player exits the house, the game will send the player to an inaccessible area north-west of Sunyshore City in the mountains, with a lake.

The map is accessible with the Tweaking glitch.
