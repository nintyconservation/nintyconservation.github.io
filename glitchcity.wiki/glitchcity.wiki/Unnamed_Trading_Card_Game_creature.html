The Unnamed Trading Card Game creature is the creature in an unused placeholder 'Pokémon' silhouette that was found in Pokémon Trading Card Game Online in 2011, alongside the Unova starter Pokémon. It could be found within a Flash file known as "TutorialNavigation".

The silhouette does not resemble that of any current Pokémon but has a face similar to Tepig. Serena Robar (community manager for The Pokémon Company International) revealed that the image is not an actual Pokémon but rather a placeholder image leftover from development.
