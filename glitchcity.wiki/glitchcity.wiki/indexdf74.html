g g is a dual-type Normal (0x11)/Glitch (0x25)-type glitch Pokémon in Pokémon Yellow.

Its family number is 000.

ゥU? (C8) (Red/Blue) is the equivalent trade glitch Pokémon in Pokémon Red and Blue.

It cannot be caught with the Trainer escape glitch or another means of instant encounter, as a (random name) (00) battle will be initiated instead.

Methods to obtain

  • Party remaining HP glitch or storage box remaining HP glitch with a remaining HP of 200.
  • Time Capsule exploit (Sentret).
  • LOL glitch (0xC8 sub-tile)
  • International fossil conversion glitch with an Attack stat of 200.
  • Equivalent trade of ゥU? (C8) from game.
  • Arbitrary code execution
