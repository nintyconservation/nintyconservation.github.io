(FE) (????? FE) is a dual-type Glitch (0x47)/B (0xFF) glitch Pokémon in Pokémon Crystal, with an index number of hex:FE (dec:254).

Attempting to capture it may result in a Glitch Dimension due to a problematic Pokédex entry, if its capture flag is not already set.

Its menu-sprites appear to differ between Gold Version and Silver Version but are still both invalid menu-sprites.

  1. Byte shifting via (00) or (FF) (available through stabilizing a bad clone or arbitrary code execution) (e.g. byte shift a total experience of 254 or 65024-65279 (unconfirmed).

Notes

When the player goes to deposit this Pokémon into a storage box, it may erroneously appear at Level 255 holding an item. Backing out of the menu may cause the game to freeze. This trait is shared with ????? (Gold/Silver FC).

This behavior does not occur for ????? (Crystal FC) or ????? (Crystal FE).
