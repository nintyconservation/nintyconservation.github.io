Return to the real world

The return to the real world glitch allows the player to return to the real world after being debrained and entering the Meat Circus.

Requirements

  1. Preferably, the player should have fully completed Lungfishopolis this is not totally necessary, but recommended, as after doing this glitch, the player will be stuck on Kocharama Island.

Procedure

  1. Use the Smelling Salts or Warp Bug to go into the Collective Unconsciousness.
  2. Enter Lungfishopolis.
  3. Go up to where the ships leading to Kocharama Island used to be.
  4. Preform a running jump off the edge of the land towards Kocharama Island. When the player is just about to hit the water, preform a double jump.
  5. After the player hits the water and the Hand of Galachio bounces the player up, the player should just make it to Kocharama island.
  6. Climb up the tower, and the series of cut-scenes that happens when the player normally clears Lungfishopolis. Keep in mind entering anyone's mind will trap the player in the Mind Worlds again. However, if the player does go to Lungfishopolis again, they will apear on Kocharama Island, where they can just climb up the radio tower.

Warp glitch

In Psychonauts, the 'warp glitch' is a glitch which involves the player appearing in the Insane Aslyum through a method not planned by the developers.

Requirements

  1. The player must not have beaten the Milkman Conspiracy.

Method

  1. Enter Boyd's mind; presuming that the player has not been in it already.
  2. The player should then use the smellingsalts or bacon to exit the Milkman Conspiracy.
  3. Enter the Milkman Conspiracy via the Brain tumbler.
  4. After beating the level and watching a cutscene the player should be in the Insane Asylum.
