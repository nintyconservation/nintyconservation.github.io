( h 4 89 is a dual-type Normal (0x91)/[D030] (0x50)-type glitch Pokémon in Pokémon Yellow.

Its family number is 202.

…… (E2) is the equivalent trade glitch Pokémon in Pokémon Red and Blue.

It cannot be caught with the Trainer escape glitch or another means of instant encounter, as a Prof. Oak battle will be initiated instead.
