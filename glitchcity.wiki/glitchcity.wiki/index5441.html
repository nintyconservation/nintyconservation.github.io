ゥ (F2) is a dual-type Flying (0x82)/[D031] (0x50)-type glitch Pokémon in Pokémon Red and Blue.

Its family number is 209.

ゥ ゥェ ゥ ▷ is the equivalent trade glitch Pokémon in Pokémon Yellow.

It cannot be caught with the Trainer escape glitch or another means of instant encounter, as a Rival (variation 2) battle will be initiated instead.
