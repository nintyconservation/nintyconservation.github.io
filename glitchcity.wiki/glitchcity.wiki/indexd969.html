Glitch Pokémon (0xEC) is a dual-type [D04A] (0xE8)/Normal (0x8E)-type glitch Pokémon in Pokémon Red and Blue.

Its family number is 211.

It has an unstable front-sprite, which may cause this glitch Pokémon to freeze the game when sent out on the opponent's side.

?/ is the equivalent trade glitch Pokémon in Pokémon Yellow.


It cannot be encountered directly with the Trainer escape glitch or another means of instant encounter, as a Lt. Surge battle will be initiated instead.
