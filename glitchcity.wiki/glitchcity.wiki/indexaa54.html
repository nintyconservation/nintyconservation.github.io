▼ pゥ is a Normal-type glitch Pokémon in Pokémon Yellow.

It is a hybrid of Snorlax.

G 'Mp (F6) is the equivalent trade glitch Pokémon in Pokémon Red and Blue.

It cannot be caught with the Trainer escape glitch or another means of instant encounter, as a Channeler battle will be initiated instead.
