The unused Bulbasaur slot machine symbol appears in Pokémon Gold and Silver for slot machines.

Normally the only possible outcomes in the Slot Machines are the "7" symbol, the Pikachu symbol, the Cherry symbol, the Pokéball symbol and the Squirtle symbol.

To force the game into considering that the player has scored three Bulbasaurs in the Slot Machine, the player can enter the GameShark codes 011809C6, 01180DC6 and 011817C6 and finish their attempt.
