Generation VI 724+ glitch Pokémon are glitch Pokémon in Pokémon X and Y, Pokémon Omega Ruby and Alpha Sapphire with index numbers of 724 through to 65535.

They are available through Nintendo 3DS firmware exploits and homebrew or other forms of hacking.

Properties

Many of these glitch Pokémon appear with Bulbasaur's model, however some of the glitch Pokémon have models of other Pokémon such as Charizard, Ivysaur, Mega Venusaur, Krabby or Arceus (Draco Plate).

Some of the glitch Pokémon can be used in battle, whereas others will freeze the game.

Additionally, all known Generation VI 724+ glitch Pokémon indices have all of their base stats as 0.

The menu sprite for a Generation VI 724+ glitch Pokémon may be the mini-sprite that is normally used when a Pokémon is loading.

These glitch Pokémon as well as two of the Generation VI glitch Egg Pokémon (#722, #723) have the ability to cause the Super Training void effect.
