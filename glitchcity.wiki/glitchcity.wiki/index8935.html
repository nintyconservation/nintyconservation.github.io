ItemDex strategy

If two glitch items have different effects and buy prices between revisions of the same game or a different name, they will still get an article (e.g. articles ItemDexJP/RG:106 (v1.0) and ItemDexJP/RG:106 (v1.1).) Otherwise they will get one page (e.g. ItemDexJP/RGB:090).
