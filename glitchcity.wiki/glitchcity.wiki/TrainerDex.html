The TrainerDex is an index of many different glitch Trainers in the Pokémon series.

The Generation I list only lists Trainer classes, however rosters for these Trainer classes can be found in their respective articles.

The Generation II list at the moment only lists individual Trainer rosters and one (semi-unused?) Trainer class.
