Pac-Man World for Game Boy Advance is a game in the Pac-Man series.

Dot trail glitch

The dot trail glitch causes the player to become frozen in position at the end of a Pac-Dot trail.

Requirements

  • Any level with a Pac-Dot trail unlocked

Procedure

  1. Set off the switch in a level that causes a Pac-Dot trail to appear.
  2. Go to the trail's destination without going to the starting point or any point along it.

The player will now be frozen in position.
