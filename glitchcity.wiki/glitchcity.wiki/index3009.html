Pk (hex:30, dec:48) is a glitch Trainer class in Pokémon Red and Blue.

The equivalent glitch Trainer in Pokémon Yellow is ’ (hex:30).

It can be fought with the Ditto trick with a Special stat of 248 (hex:F8).
