ゥU? is a dual-type PokéManiac (0x61)/Glitch (0x27) glitch Pokémon in Pokémon Red and Blue.

Its family number is 234.

g g is the equivalent trade glitch Pokémon in Pokémon Yellow.

ゥU? cannot be caught with the Trainer escape glitch or another means of instant encounter, as glitch Trainer class 0x00 ('Jacred') will appear instead.

Methods to obtain

  • Party remaining HP glitch or storage box remaining HP glitch with a remaining HP of 200.
  • Time Capsule exploit (Sentret).
  • CoolTrainer♀ corruption (0xC8 sub-tile).
  • LOL glitch (0xC8 sub-tile)
  • International fossil conversion glitch with an Attack stat of 200.
  • Double distort CoolTrainer♀ corruption (0xC8 sub-tile, normally cannot be input)
  • Equivalent trade of g g from game.
  • Arbitrary code execution
