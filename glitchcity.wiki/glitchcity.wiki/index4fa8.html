00 (????? 00) is a Steel-type glitch Pokémon in Pokémon Gold and Silver (French), with an index number of hex:00 (dec:00).


Methods to obtain

  1. Stabilize a bad clone.
  2. Arbitrary code execution (e.g. through wrong pocket TMs, glitch Pokédex categories).

If the Hall of Fame SRAM glitch functions properly on this version, this glitch may be another option.

Pokédex entry

(Appears to be the same as the English equivalent)

Notes

This glitch Pokémon may force a Link Battle against a poisoned version of a ????? upon encounter with excessive HP.

If it is captured, a glitch Trainer may send out a Raichu with the "BRU" (Brûlure) status ailment (in Pokémon Gold) or an unknown Pokémon with the "PSN" (Empoisonné) status (in Pokémon Silver).
