Chief (hex:1B, dec:27) (Japanese: シルフのチーフ, Silph's Chief or Silph's Manager) is an unused Trainer class in Pokémon Red, Blue, and Yellow and glitch Trainer.

A Chief can be fought with the Ditto trick in Pokémon Red, Blue, Yellow with a Special stat of 227 (hex:E3), or via the old man trick with the "-" (hex:E3) character in Pokémon Red and Blue.

The Chief has no defined rosters, so their rosters from an instant encounter glitch such as the Ditto trick are taken from the next Trainer class; Scientist. Rosters that proceed are invalid and may (after covering all valid Trainer rosters) eventually consist of glitch Pokémon.

For the old man trick, Chief's rosters are taken from that of the (random name) (hex:00) glitch Trainer instead.

Notes

The hidden Safari Zone text mentioning Silph's Manager in Japanese Pokémon Red/Green/Blue.

In Celadon City there is an NPC who mentions a "Chief", bragging that Team Rocket has "just shipped 2000 Pokémon as slot prizes".

Furthermore there is a semi-unused sentence in the game that says "SILPH's manager is hiding in the SAFARI ZONE." (Japanese: シルフのチーフが サファりゾーンの どこかに かくれてるんだって!). This for unknown reasons can be accessed as one of the hidden Safari Zone texts by talking to a chair in one of the Safari Zone rest houses in Japanese Pokémon Red, Green, and Pokémon Blue (Japanese).
