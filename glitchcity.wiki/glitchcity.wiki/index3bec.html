ゥ₽'M is a dual-type Glitch (0x21)/Glitch (0x9D)-type glitch Pokémon in Pokémon Red and Blue.

Its family number is 224.

It has an unstable front-sprite, which may cause this glitch Pokémon to freeze the game when sent out on the opponent's side.

When sound bank fixes are applied, its behavior is identical to 4 4, but it crashes after it partially shows up.

h 4 pゥ ゥ… is the equivalent trade glitch Pokémon in Pokémon Yellow.

The front-sprite of this glitch Pokémon freezes the game. Its backsprite varies and has a chance of freezing the game.

It cannot be encountered directly with the Trainer escape glitch or another means of instant encounter, as a Blaine battle will be initiated instead.
