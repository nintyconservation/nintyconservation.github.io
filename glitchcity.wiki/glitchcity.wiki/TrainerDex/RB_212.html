Battling with TM12 (D4)

TM12 (hex:D4) is a glitch Trainer class in Pokémon Red and Blue and one of the artificial Trainer classes.

Game Genie codes D4F-549-C49 and 3EF-539-B31, combined with an encounter will allow the player to face it.

Trainer AI functions

Move modification AI routines

  • $FA58 (move choice modification #$10)
  • $0100 (move choice modification #$A5)
  • $2157 (move choice modification #$54)


Structure 2 routine

  • $7E95

Observations:

  • Has been observed loading offgao's memory editor, which was stored on this save file at $FA80 ($DA80).
