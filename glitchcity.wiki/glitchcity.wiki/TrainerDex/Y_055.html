ゥ, A (hex:37, dec:055) is a glitch Trainer class in Pokémon Yellow.

The equivalent glitch Trainer in Pokémon Red and Blue is 14S (hex:37).

It can be fought with the Ditto trick with a Special stat of 255 (hex:FF).

ゥ, A has an arbitrary sprite sourced from E891 (C891), which is a temporary part of the C6E8 (wOverworldMap) buffer. With arbitrary code execution, it is possible to change the sprite into anything the player desires (within technical constraints). This exploit also applies to 14S in Red/Blue.
