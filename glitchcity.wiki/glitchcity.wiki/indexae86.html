O PkMn4 X is a dual-type Bug/Poison-type glitch Pokémon in Pokémon Red and Blue.

It is a hybrid of Beedrill.

4, ゥァ (CF) is the equivalent trade glitch Pokémon in Pokémon Yellow.

It cannot be caught with the Trainer escape glitch or another means of instant encounter, as a Pokémaniac battle will be initiated instead.

Methods to obtain

  • Party remaining HP glitch or storage box remaining HP glitch with a remaining HP of 207.
  • Time Capsule exploit (Ariados).
  • CoolTrainer♀ corruption (0xCF sub-tile).
  • LOL glitch (0xCF sub-tile)
  • International fossil conversion glitch with an Attack stat of 207.
  • Double distort CoolTrainer♀ corruption (0xCF sub-tile, normally cannot be input)
  • Equivalent trade of 4, ゥァ (CF) from Pokémon Yellow.
  • Arbitrary code execution
