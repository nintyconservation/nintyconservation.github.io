'N g 'Mp is a dual-type Normal/Flying-type glitch Pokémon in Pokémon Red and Blue.

It is a hybrid of Pidgeot.

PkMn (F7) is the equivalent trade glitch Pokémon in Pokémon Yellow.

It cannot be caught with the Trainer escape glitch or another means of instant encounter, as a Lance battle will be initiated instead.

Methods to obtain

  • Party remaining HP glitch or storage box remaining HP glitch with a remaining HP of 247.
  • Time Capsule exploit (Steelix).
  • CoolTrainer♀ corruption ("1" character).
  • LOL glitch ("1" character)
  • International fossil conversion glitch with an Attack stat of 247.
  • Double distort CoolTrainer♀ corruption ("1" character)
  • Equivalent trade of PkMn (F7) from Pokémon Yellow.
  • Arbitrary code execution
