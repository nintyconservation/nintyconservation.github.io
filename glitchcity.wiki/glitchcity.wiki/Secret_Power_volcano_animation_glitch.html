The Secret Power volcano animation glitch is a glitch in Generation VII.

Summary

When Secret Power is used in a volcanic area, it will have Incinerate's animation (a move which normally affects more than one user).

If the move is used when multiple opponents are on the field, all Pokémon will react as if they were hit, even though the move only targets one Pokémon.
