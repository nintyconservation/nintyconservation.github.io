This database, known as the TMHMDex documents the effects of wrong pocket TMs and HMs in Pokémon Gold, Silver and Crystal.

These are possible to obtain with glitches which include the duplicate key items glitch and arbitrary code execution.

Thanks luckytyphlosion for the effect pointers.
