TM08 is a (variable name) (0x23)-type glitch move in Pokémon Red, Blue, and Yellow. It is not capable of causing Super Glitch.

Successfully using this glitch move may result in damage with Scratch's animation. If the move misses, 1 HP damage is also dealt to the user.
