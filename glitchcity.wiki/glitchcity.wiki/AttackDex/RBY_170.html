(Variable name) is a Normal-type glitch move in Pokémon Red, Blue, and Yellow. It is capable of causing Super Glitch.

As it is capable of causing Super Glitch, having a 0x50 sub-tile early in the saved screen data may be required to avoid its Super Glitch effects.

Ignoring Super Glitch, this glitch move will function like Dream Eater in that it will always miss if the opponent is not asleep, but only has 6 base power. This glitch move has Doubleslap's animation.
