♀ . (C1) is a dual-type Normal (0x93)/Normal (0x80)-type glitch Pokémon in Pokémon Yellow.

Its family number is 205.

ゥ (C1) is the equivalent trade glitch Pokémon in Pokémon Red and Blue.

Sometimes the sprite of this glitch Pokémon will corrupt the music when it is encountered on the opponent's side, although this does not always occur, possibly due to varying VRAM data and/or VRAM inaccessibility.
