'M (FF) is a dual-type Fire/Flying-type glitch Pokémon in Pokémon Red and Blue.

It is a hybrid of Charizard. As a result, it is often referred to as "Charizard 'M".

It cannot be caught with the Trainer escape glitch or another means of instant encounter, as a "14S" (0x37) glitch Trainer battle will be initiated instead.

Q is the equivalent trade glitch Pokémon in Pokémon Yellow.

Methods to obtain

  • CoolTrainer♀ corruption ("9" character, e.g. from Safari Zone exit glitch Route 1 Glitch City).
  • LOL glitch ("9" character)
  • Equivalent trade of Q from Pokémon Yellow.
  • International fossil conversion glitch with an Attack stat of 255.
  • Double distort CoolTrainer♀ corruption ("9" character, normally cannot be entered)
  • Pokémon Bank hex:FF glitch Pokémon glitch (Virtual Console version only)
  • Arbitrary code execution
