4 4 is a dual-type Glitch (0x39)/Normal-type glitch Pokémon in Pokémon Yellow.

Its family number is 250.

▶ A (BF) is the equivalent trade glitch Pokémon in Pokémon Red and Blue.

Attempting to battle with 4 4 via the Trainer escape glitch, or another means of instant encounter, or 4 4 appearing on the opponent's side will most likely result in a freeze.

  • LOL glitch ('v character)
