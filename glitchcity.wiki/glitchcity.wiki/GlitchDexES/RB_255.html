0 'MÙ Ù is a dual-type Fire/Flying-type glitch Pokémon in Pokémon Red and Blue (Spanish).

It is a hybrid of Charizard.

It cannot be caught with the Trainer escape glitch or another means of instant encounter, as a glitch Trainer battle will be initiated instead.

ÑQ Í is the equivalent trade glitch Pokémon in Pokémon Yellow.
