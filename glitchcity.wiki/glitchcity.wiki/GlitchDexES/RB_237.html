áhÄ ゥ Ó is a dual-type Glitch/Glitch type glitch Pokémon in Pokémon Red and Blue (Spanish).

] óhÄ4ᴘÍ? ▶ is the equivalent trade glitch Pokémon in Pokémon Yellow.

It cannot be caught with the Trainer escape glitch or another means of instant encounter, as a Koga battle will be initiated instead.
