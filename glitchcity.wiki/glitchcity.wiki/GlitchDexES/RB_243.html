ゥ Ó ÙM (0xF3) is an Electric-type glitch Pokémon in Pokémon Red and Blue (Spanish).

It is a hybrid of Raichu.

ゥ▶È ゥ▼È ゥ is the equivalent trade glitch Pokémon in Pokémon Yellow.

It cannot be caught with the Trainer escape glitch or another means of instant encounter, as a Rival (champion variation) battle will be initiated instead.

This glitch Pokémon's Pokédex entry is capable of partial battle escape.
