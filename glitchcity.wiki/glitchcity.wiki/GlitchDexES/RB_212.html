Ù Aゥ òÓÙf is a dual-type Ghost/Poison type glitch Pokémon in Pokémon Red and Blue (Spanish).

It is a hybrid of Gengar.

Ù Ù ゥ is the equivalent trade glitch Pokémon in Pokémon Yellow.

It cannot be caught with the Trainer escape glitch or another means of instant encounter, as an Engineer battle will be initiated instead.

Methods to obtain

  • Party remaining HP glitch or storage box remaining HP glitch with a remaining HP of 212.
  • CoolTrainer♀ corruption (0xD4 sub-tile).
  • LOL glitch (0xD4 sub-tile)
  • International fossil conversion glitch with an Attack stat of 212.
  • Double distort CoolTrainer♀ corruption (0xD4 sub-tile)
  • Equivalent trade of Ù Ù ゥ from Pokémon Yellow.
