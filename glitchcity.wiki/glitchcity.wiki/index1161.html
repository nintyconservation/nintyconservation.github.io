8 P ァ is a dual-type Normal (0x11)/Normal-type glitch Pokémon in Pokémon Yellow.

Its family number is 255.

/PkMn ▼PkMn (E0) is the equivalent trade glitch Pokémon in Pokémon Red and Blue.

It cannot be caught with the Trainer escape glitch or another means of instant encounter, as a Blackbelt battle will be initiated instead.

  • Party remaining HP glitch or storage box remaining HP glitch with a remaining HP of 224.
  • Time Capsule exploit (Sudowoodo).
  • LOL glitch (' (apostrophe) character)
  • International fossil conversion glitch with an Attack stat of 224.
  • Equivalent trade of /PkMn ▼PkMn (E0) from Pokémon Red and Blue.
