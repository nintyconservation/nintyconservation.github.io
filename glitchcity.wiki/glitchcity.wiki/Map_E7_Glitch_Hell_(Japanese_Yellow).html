Map E7 Glitch Hell, also known as Map E7 Fiery Glitch Hell is a descent glitch map only found in the Japanese version of Pokémon Yellow.

Accessing map E7 through the modification of D2E4 (item 34 in the expanded items pack) places Red into a red glitch map with a black and white Red which forces the player to scroll down, similar to the cycling based glitch maps in English Yellow.

In v1.0, the player scrolls through the map on a red screen as a 'burned' Red. In v1.1+, the player scrolls through the map with a black background and for a moment Red may turn into a gentleman and disappear.

  • The glitch map in version 1.0, notably red with the default palette.

  • The glitch map in versions 1.1 and above, notably black.
