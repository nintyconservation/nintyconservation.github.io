ゥゥ] is a Normal-type glitch Pokémon in Pokémon Yellow.

It is a hybrid of Tauros and one of the Ragon Pokédex entry glitch Pokémon.

M p'u ゥ is the equivalent trade glitch Pokémon in Pokémon Red and Blue.

It cannot be caught with the Trainer escape glitch or another means of instant encounter, as a Burglar battle will be initiated instead.

  • Party remaining HP glitch or storage box remaining HP glitch with a remaining HP of 211.

  • LOL glitch (0xD3 sub-tile)
  • International fossil conversion glitch with an Attack stat of 211.
