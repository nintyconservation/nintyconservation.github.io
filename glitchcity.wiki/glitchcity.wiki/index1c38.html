The Life Orb glitch is a glitch in Pokémon Battle Revolution.

If the opponent's current Pokémon holds a Life Orb and uses Heat Wave the Pokémon may faint from the Life Orb damage before using the move. Immediately after the Pokémon faints, the move is performed, which makes it appear as if no Pokémon had performed the move.
