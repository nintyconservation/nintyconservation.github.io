Glitch Pokémon (0xE3) is a dual-type [D04A] (0x7B)/[D04A] (0x70)-type glitch Pokémon in Pokémon Red and Blue.

Its family number is 240.

4 89 4 is the equivalent trade glitch Pokémon in Pokémon Yellow.

This glitch Pokémon has a name that is terminated with 0x50 before it begins, essentially giving it a 'blank' name when the name has to be displayed. However, other characters exist after the 0x50 byte.

It cannot be caught with the Trainer escape glitch or another means of instant encounter, as a Chief battle will be initiated instead.
