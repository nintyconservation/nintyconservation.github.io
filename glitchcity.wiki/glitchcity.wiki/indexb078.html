▶ A is a dual-type Normal (0x0B)/Normal-type glitch Pokémon in Pokémon Red and Blue.

Its family number is 250.

4 4 is the equivalent trade glitch Pokémon in Pokémon Yellow.

Methods to obtain

  • Ditto trick (191 Special)
  • Equivalent trade of 4 4 from Pokémon Yellow.
  • Party remaining HP glitch or storage box remaining HP glitch with a remaining HP of 191.
  • Time Capsule exploit (Chikorita)
  • CoolTrainer♀ corruption ('v character)
  • LOL glitch ('v character)
  • International fossil conversion glitch with an Attack stat of 191.
  • Double distort CoolTrainer♀ corruption ('v character)
  • Old man trick ('v character; cannot normally be input)
