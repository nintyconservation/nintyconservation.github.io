ゥ▼ ‖ゥ♂ is a Fire-type glitch Pokémon in Pokémon Yellow.

It is a hybrid of Magmar.

Glitch Pokémon (FA) is the equivalent trade glitch Pokémon in Pokémon Red and Blue.

It cannot be caught with the Trainer escape glitch or another means of instant encounter, as a "4 8 ) (32)" glitch Trainer battle will be initiated instead.

Methods to obtain

  • Party remaining HP glitch or storage box remaining HP glitch with a remaining HP of 250.
  • Time Capsule exploit (Qwilfish).
  • LOL glitch ("4" character)
  • International fossil conversion glitch with an Attack stat of 250.
  • Evolves from 4 4 Hy (C0) at Level 239.
  • Evolves from g g (C8) at Level 201.
  • Evolves from 8 (DE) at Level 201.
  • Evolves from p (DF) at Level 36.
  • Evolves from h 4 pゥ ゥ… (EF) at Level 201.
