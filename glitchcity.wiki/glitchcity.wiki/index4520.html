Q is a dual-type Water/Psychic-type glitch Pokémon in Pokémon Yellow.

It is a hybrid of Starmie.

It cannot be caught with the Trainer escape glitch or another means of instant encounter, as a "ゥ, A" (0x37) glitch Trainer battle will be initiated instead.

'M (FF) is the equivalent trade glitch Pokémon in Pokémon Red and Blue.

Methods to obtain

  • Equivalent trade of 'M (FF) from Pokémon Red and Blue.
  • LOL glitch ("9" character)
  • International fossil conversion glitch with an Attack stat of 255.
  • Evolve 4 4Hy (C0) at Level 6.
  • Evolve 8 (DE) at Level 6.
  • Pokémon Bank hex:FF glitch Pokémon glitch (Virtual Console version only)
  • Arbitrary code execution
