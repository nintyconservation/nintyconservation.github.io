ゥ▶ ゥ▼ ゥ (F3) is a dual-type Normal/[D030] (0x50)-type glitch Pokémon in Pokémon Yellow.

Its family number is 195.

ゥ (F3) is the equivalent trade glitch Pokémon in Pokémon Red and Blue.

It cannot be caught with the Trainer escape glitch or another means of instant encounter, as a Rival champion battle will be initiated instead.
