ゥ 4 4 is a dual-type Ice/Flying-type glitch Pokémon in Pokémon Yellow.

It is a hybrid of Articuno, and one of the scrambled Bulbasaur glitch Pokémon.

'Ng ゥ₽ (F8) is the equivalent trade glitch Pokémon in Pokémon Red and Blue.

It cannot be caught with the Trainer escape glitch or another means of instant encounter, as a "’ (30)" glitch Trainer battle will be initiated instead.
