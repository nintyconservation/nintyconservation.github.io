ネ＿ノﾞ バ is a glitch Pokémon in the leaked Pokémon Gold and Silver Nintendo Space World 1997 demos, with an unused Egg front sprite.

Much like Generation II glitch Egg and the Fossil/Ghost MissingNo. from Generation I, many of its stats vary based on the previous Pokémon in memory.

Notes

Attempting to battle it as an opponent may lock up the game with glitch audio in the Gold debug version. A glitchy cry for this glitch Pokémon has also been observed in Silver debug but it hasn't been observed to lock up the game. Freezes have not been observed on the non-debug versions.
