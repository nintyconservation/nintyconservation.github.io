4( h 4 ? (ED) is a Normal-type glitch Pokémon in Pokémon Yellow.

Its family number is 159.

It is the only total items changing glitch Pokémon in English Pokémon Red, Blue, and Yellow, but its sprite on the opponent's side may freeze the game.

Furthermore it is affected by the experience division by 0 glitch from its experience group, 0xFF. The only known methods to obtain this glitch Pokémon include arbitrary code execution, finding it in the expanded party or obtaining a hybrid of this glitch Pokémon with 4( h 4 ? (ED) as the donor (e.g. via trade).

h ゥ (ED) is the equivalent trade glitch Pokémon in Pokémon Red and Blue.

It cannot be found directly with the Trainer escape glitch or another means of instant encounter, as an Erika battle will be initiated instead.
