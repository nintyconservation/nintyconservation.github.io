PkMn (D8) is a dual-type Normal (0x93)/Normal (0x80)-type glitch Pokémon in Pokémon Yellow.

Its family number is 205.

PkMnaPkMnゥ ♂ fPkMnk (D8) is the equivalent trade glitch Pokémon in Pokémon Red and Blue.

It cannot be caught with the Trainer escape glitch or another means of instant encounter, as a Cue Ball battle will be initiated instead.

Its known evolutions are shared with ゥ (D4).

  • Party remaining HP glitch or storage box remaining HP glitch with a remaining HP of 216.

  • International fossil conversion glitch with an Attack stat of 216.
